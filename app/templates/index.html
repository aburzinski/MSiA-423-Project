Who's in First?

A Major League Baseball Awards Predictor

This website predicts who is likely to win Major League Baseball's Most Valuable Player and Cy Young Awards. The page below lists the top 10 players most likely to win each award as of today. Statistical data for this website is updated daily. The website uses this current data along with data going back to 1960 to make each award prediction. Click on a player's name to see detail statistics displayed using a radar chart. You can also click on a team name (either next to the player or from the team list at the bottom of the screen) to see how any player on any team is predicted to do when it comes to winning Major League baseball's most prestigious awards.
